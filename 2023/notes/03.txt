Advent of Code 2023 Day 3: Gear Ratios
https://adventofcode.com/2023/day/3

Fun: 1/5
Difficulty: 2/5 (implementation)

For this problem we simply need to scan the grid to find the numbers, and
check the adjacent cells for symbols. Then for part 1, we sum up the numbers
that are adjacent to a symbol. For part 2, we multiply the pairs of numbers
that are adjacent to the same '*' symbol.
